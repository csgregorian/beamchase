A growing number of teenagers spend less and less time outdoors.

At Intelliplay, we aim to solve this national problem and to provide an organic experience by minimizing the augmented reality associated with technology. Our product, BeamChase, will place an emphasis on rekindling the wonders of nature to Canadian youth by bringing the traditional game of indoor laser tag to the great outdoors. With the use of infrared technology, a phone attachment and an app, players will be able to interact with each other while immersing themselves in the outdoor world.
